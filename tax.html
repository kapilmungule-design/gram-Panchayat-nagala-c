  • ✔ New Water Connection Charges

Tax Payment Process

1. Visit Panchayat Office

Submit property or water connection details.

2. Tax Verification

Officials verify your tax information.

3. Make Payment

Pay tax amount and collect official receipt.

Required Documents

  • 📄 Aadhaar Card
  • 📄 Property Documents
  • 📄 Previous Tax Receipt
  • 📄 Mobile Number
  • 📄 Water Connection Details

Important Tax Rules

  • Tax should be paid before the due date.
  • Keep your payment receipt safely.
  • Update property information if required.
  • Contact Gram Panchayat for correction.
