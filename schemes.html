General Eligibility

  • ✔ Applicant should be an Indian Citizen.
  • ✔ Aadhaar Card should be available.
  • ✔ Bank Account linked with Aadhaar.
  • ✔ Mobile Number should be active.
  • ✔ Income and category conditions apply according to scheme.

Yes, many schemes support online application through government portals.
